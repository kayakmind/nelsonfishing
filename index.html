I offer handmade amadou fly drying patches at an affordable price. I can also engrave your organization’s logo on patches.

These fly dryers are constructed of the finest quality amadou, a natural material obtained from a tree mushroom know a the horse’s hoof fungus (Fomes fomentacius). Keeping your fly dry is key to keeping it floating and visible when fishing.

Amadou has an unbelievable absorbent capacity and it the best natural material for drying flies, especially flies made with CDC.

I hand make each of these from pigskin leather and amadou harvested in Europe. The dryer patches measure 1 3/4 inches by 2 inches.

They vary in thickness, depending on the amadou harvest and are much thicker than the others on the market.
